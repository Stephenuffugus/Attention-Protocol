#### B. Y Combinator's Work at a Startup
- **URL:** https://www.workatastartup.com
- **Best for:** YC-portfolio company contract roles. They love YC-applicant bios. Bonus: doesn't hurt your YC application. Partners often see who's active here.
- **Time-to-first-money:** 1 to 3 weeks
- **Cost:** Free

  3. YC startups often hire fractional engineering leads on direct contract. The trajectory bio above is precisely what they want.

#### C. Upwork
- **URL:** https://www.upwork.com
- **Best for:** Fast cash, JS/Firebase/cryptography contract work. Lowest barrier to entry.
- **Time-to-first-money:** 1 to 7 days for fast applicants
- **Cost:** Free; about 10% platform fee on earnings
- **Steps:**
  1. Sign up as a *Freelancer*. Profile, paste trajectory bio (medium length).
  2. Take the free skills-test for "JavaScript" + "Node.js" + "Firebase." They boost your profile rank.
  3. Set hourly rate: **$80 to $150/hr.** You'll have to climb from there as a new profile.
  4. Use Connects (free credits given on signup) to apply to 5 gigs in the first session. Best categories:
     - "Firebase / Firestore consulting"
     - "Cryptography / signing / JWKS"
     - "Web app / SaaS architecture review"
  5. **First gig is the hardest.** Get one $500 to $1000 project at a lower rate to build profile rating, then climb.
- **Tip:** Reply to job posts within 1 hour of posting. The first 5 applicants get 80% of the responses.
