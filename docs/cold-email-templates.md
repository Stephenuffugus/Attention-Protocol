## Follow-up sequencing (all templates)

**Day 0:** send the cold email with evidence kit attached.

**Day 4:** if no reply, one short follow-up:

> Hi [First] — bumping this up. The 10-minute self-qualification
> evidence kit is still attached; if it's the wrong person on your side,
> I'd appreciate a pointer to whoever owns [Part 11 training / CME
> accreditation outcomes / credentialing platform standards]. Thanks.

**Day 11:** if no reply, last attempt:

> Last note. Six months from now half of pharma compliance
> conversations will mention cryptographic attention attestation.
> [Company] can lead that or follow it. If you want to discuss leading
> it, I'm here.
>
> Stephen

No more follow-ups after that. Move to the next target.
