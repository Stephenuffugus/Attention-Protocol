## Likely objections + rebuttals

1. **"We already have Veeva Vault or ComplianceWire tracking completion."**
   Rebuttal: Those systems answer *did someone click through?*. They do not answer *did a human actually engage, versus a bot, shared login, or GPT-assisted session?*. Our receipt answers that — and an FDA inspector can verify it offline without calling us. That's the new bar post-H2-2025 enforcement.

2. **"What's your SOC 2 / ISO 27001 posture?"**
   Rebuttal (honest): Not certified yet. Solo founder, patent-pending, pre-Series A. I provide the compliance matrix and security architecture docs, and pilot under a tight MSA with Merck's security team. Pilot is a 60-day measurement exercise, not a production system deployment.

3. **"How do you detect bots?"**
   Rebuttal: We don't pitch a single-number classifier. The receipt carries layered evidence — environmental gate (BotD), honeypot, composition integrity, behavioral composite. An auditor reviews layers; no single number is the verdict.
