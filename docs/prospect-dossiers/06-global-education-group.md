## Likely objections + rebuttals

1. **"Primary care learners complete activities on mobile, on-the-go. Will this work?"**
   Rebuttal: The behavioral composite works across touch and mouse input. Environmental signals (BotD) run in-browser regardless of device. Pilot scope can explicitly include a mobile-majority activity to produce data on this.

2. **"We already report outcomes in Moore's Levels 3–5."**
   Rebuttal: Moore's Levels measure knowledge/competence/performance/health outcomes downstream of the activity. SWS's receipt is upstream — integrity of engagement during the activity. Complementary measurement, not replacement.

3. **"How do you detect bots? Our fraud-scoring vendor claims 99%."**
   Rebuttal: We don't pitch a single-number classifier. The receipt carries layered evidence — environmental, honeypot, composition, behavioral composite. An auditor reviews the layers. Bot-vs-human composite gap alone is modest; the value is cryptographic verifiability of the full evidence stack.
