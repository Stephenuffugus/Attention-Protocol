## Likely objections + rebuttals

1. **"Our video player already tracks play events."**
   Rebuttal: Play events tell you the video was playing. They don't tell you the learner was actually looking at or interacting with the screen. SWS's behavioral composite runs in parallel, carries environmental and composition signals, and produces a signed receipt the grantor can verify offline. Complementary, not replacement.

2. **"Does ACCME accept this?"**
   Rebuttal (honest): ACCME has not endorsed any cryptographic attestation vendor. What they did in December 2025 is open the category. Being first in video-heavy oncology CME is a competitive position, not a compliance requirement.

3. **"How do you handle asynchronous learners on mobile?"**
   Rebuttal: Behavioral composite works across touch and mouse input. Mobile is explicitly in scope for pilot design. The evidence kit includes device-class breakdown guidance.
