# YC S26 Pitch Positioning Brief

Source: agent research synthesis 2026-04-26. Use this to shape the YC application and partner conversations.

## Recommended One-Liner

> **"Cryptographic proof-of-human-attention for the agentic web — a SHA-256 receipt every regulated transaction (CME credentialing first) can attach as evidence that a real human did the work, not an AI agent or bot."**

Three load-bearing parts:
1. **Cryptographic receipt** — the differentiated artifact nobody else ships
2. **Agentic web framing** — matches the W26 dominant narrative (Didit, Roundtable Proof of Human, World AgentKit)
3. **CME credentialing wedge** — one regulated buyer, regulatory tailwind, no entrenched competitor

## Direct W26 Comparables

YC explicitly accepted two AI-agent-era proof-of-humanness companies in W26 — meaning **the slot is open and YC is paying for it**:

- **Didit (W26)** — "All-in-one identity platform for fast human verification. Distinguish real humans from bots and AI agents with minimal friction." $2M pre-YC, +20% MoM growth, identical-twin founders (personal-pain framing). Stack: ID + biometrics + liveness + auth, built from scratch.
- **Roundtable Proof of Human (W26)** — "API that invisibly detects bots and verifies human identity." 2 ex-Princeton cognitive scientists.

**Differentiation vs Roundtable:** they sell a score (invisible API). SWS sells a receipt (portable artifact). They frame as cognitive-science credentialing; we frame as cryptographic evidence.

## Founder-Pain Framing

Winning W26 stories led with personal pain (Didit twins, Roundtable cognitive-scientist origin). For SWS:

> "I have a daughter. I want to leave her a trustworthy internet. The system I built started as a way to prove my own attention to a publisher who paid me by the page-view — now it's the only public protocol that gives any auditor a cryptographic receipt of human-vs-AI work."

Anchor on solo-founder + daughter (long-term provision) + the patent + the live ledger. The technical depth answers the "is this just vibes" objection.

## Demo-Day Hook

A live demo where the partner takes the test on their phone, sees their own SHA-256 receipt land in the public ledger in front of them, and then watches a known bot from the adversarial harness produce a different receipt with a "PASSIVE — bot suspected" verdict. The receipt is the product; show the product.
