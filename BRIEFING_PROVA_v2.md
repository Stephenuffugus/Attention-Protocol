Pfizer / Lilly / BMS / AstraZeneca / Genentech all score "measurement of learner progression" harder than they did in 2023.

The accreditation chain is the same as for psychiatry (PPP-shape) and oncology (CEC-shape):

```
ACCME (the accreditor)
  ↓ accredits
MECs like Prova, CEC, Medscape, DKBmed, PeerView
  ↓ propose to
Pharma grantors
  ↓ fund accredited activities for
Specialty physicians + interprofessional team
```

Prova's competitive position: mid-size, multi-specialty, outcomes-rigor as the moat. Their competition for the same grants is mostly larger MECs (Medscape, DKBmed) plus newer specialty boutiques.

The receipt is "the next layer underneath outcomes data" — not a replacement, an augmentation. Rob will hear this framing as a respect signal.
