The Experiment

Step 1: Stay on this page and read this content for 30 seconds. Scroll around. Click things. Your SWS Focus Score will climb because you're deeply engaged.

Step 2: Switch to another tab for 30 seconds, then come back. Your Focus Score will drop because SWS knows you weren't paying attention — even though GA4's engagement timer keeps running.

The point: GA4 will report identical "engagement time" for both scenarios. SWS reports dramatically different Focus Scores. One number tells the truth. The other doesn't.
