20 Behavioral Science Signals

Each signal is backed by decades of peer-reviewed research. Together, they produce a Human Confidence Score that distinguishes genuine human attention from automation with 95%+ accuracy.

Timing Entropy

Human inter-event timing follows log-normal distribution (CV 0.4-1.5). Bots produce near-zero variance.

Fitts' Law

Movement time scales logarithmically with target distance. Humans physically cannot violate this 70-year law. Bots routinely do.

Hick's Law

Decision time increases with number of choices. Humans take longer with more options. Bots respond in constant time.

Scroll Saccade

Human reading produces fixation pauses during scrolling (300-3000ms). Bots scroll at constant velocity with zero pauses.

Micro-Pause

200-600ms cognitive processing delay between content render and first interaction. Bots interact within 0-50ms.

Touch Variance

Human finger contact area varies naturally between taps (angle, pressure, position). Automated taps produce identical values.
