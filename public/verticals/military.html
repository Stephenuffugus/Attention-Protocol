The Problem: Every Analytics Tool Requires Content Access

You need to verify that personnel genuinely engaged with required training and briefing materials. But every analytics and attention measurement tool on the market requires access to content, URLs, or user identifiers — making them categorically ineligible for classified environments.

So you're stuck with manual observation, sign-in sheets, and LMS timestamps that prove presence but not engagement. A service member who opens a briefing, minimizes the window, and signs the sheet has "completed" the training. The system cannot tell the difference.
