A seven-layer signed receipt
Each layer is attached to the same record, covered by the same signature, and disclosed separately. A buyer combines them per their policy — strict, lenient, or regulatory.
01

Environmental

7-vector multi-detector: BotD v2 + WebGL renderer (cloud-VM signature) + WebGPU adapter info (ACM WiSec 2025) + Function.toString consistency + iframe-frontier comparison + chrome.runtime presence + AudioContext prototype-shape. Naive Puppeteer caught at suspicion 0.750. Disclosed separately from behavioral data.

02

Behavioral

23 peer-reviewed signals (20 weighted + 3 diagnostic-only) — Fitts' Law, Hick's Law, prewhitened DFA-1 1/f scaling (Torre-Delignières 2008), two-thirds power law, reading-speed coherence, keystroke digraph timing, micro-pause cadence, Crossman-Goodeve / Meyer 1988 submovement count, Engbert-Kliegl 2003 microsaccades — produce a composite engagement score plus a calibrated Bayesian P(human), not a bot/not-bot verdict.

03

Composition Integrity

Paste-burst + no-backspace + digraph-interval detector. Catches LLM-assisted answers that survive the other layers. Research basis: arxiv 2511.12468 (97-99% F1).

04

Consent

Explicit user authorization recorded with ISO-8601 timestamp, version, and category list. Covered by the integrity hash — tampering breaks verification. GDPR Article 7, CCPA §1798.120 compliant by schema.

05

Ed25519 Signature

RFC 8032 digital signature over the full VC-JWT. Public key at /.well-known/attention-pubkey.json. Verifiable offline in any language, any browser, forever.

06

OpenTimestamps

Bitcoin blockchain anchor via public calendar servers. Once confirmed, the receipt is provably as-old-as a specific Bitcoin block. Backdating requires re-mining Bitcoin.
