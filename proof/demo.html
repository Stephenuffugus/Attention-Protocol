Section 1: Coverage Overview

This policy provides comprehensive liability coverage for all attention verification services deployed by the policyholder. Coverage includes but is not limited to: behavioral signal collection, cryptographic hash generation, quality tier classification, and attention receipt issuance across all supported platforms and devices.

The policyholder is covered for up to $2,000,000 per occurrence and $5,000,000 in aggregate annual liability arising from attention verification activities conducted in accordance with this policy's terms.

Section 2: Exclusions

The following are explicitly excluded from coverage: (a) Services deployed without proper user consent mechanisms as defined in Section 7.2; (b) Attention data collected from minors under 13 years of age without COPPA-compliant parental consent; (c) Any use of attention verification technology for surveillance, employee monitoring without disclosure, or law enforcement profiling purposes.

Additionally excluded: (d) Attention verification conducted on medical devices requiring FDA 510(k) clearance unless separately endorsed; (e) Use in autonomous vehicle control systems; (f) Military applications in non-allied territories without written DoD authorization.

Section 3: Claims Process

To file a claim under this policy, the policyholder must provide: (1) The cryptographic attention receipts for the disputed session; (2) Server-side verification logs showing receipt validation timestamps; (3) The behavioral signal breakdown demonstrating the quality tier classification that was applied. Claims must be filed within 90 days of the incident date.

The insurer reserves the right to independently verify attention receipts using the protocol's public verification endpoint. Disputed quality tier classifications will be adjudicated by a panel of three independent behavioral science experts, with the policyholder bearing the cost of one expert and the insurer bearing the cost of two.

Section 4: Premium Calculation

Premiums are calculated based on: total monthly verified attention events, average quality tier distribution (policies with higher deep-focus ratios receive preferred rates), historical claim frequency, and the policyholder's session integrity score. Policyholders maintaining a 95%+ session integrity score for 12 consecutive months qualify for the Verified Attention Discount of 15%.

Section 5: Renewal and Cancellation

This policy automatically renews annually unless cancelled in writing 60 days prior to the renewal date. The insurer may cancel this policy with 30 days written notice if the policyholder's session integrity score falls below 70% for three consecutive months, or if the policyholder is found to have generated fraudulent attention receipts.
