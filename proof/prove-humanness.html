Proof of Humanness

Prove you're a real human during a specific interaction — posting, signing up, bidding, submitting a policy comment, joining a forum — without revealing your identity. No iris scan. No phone number. No social graph. No deposit. Just a three-minute behavioral session that produces a cryptographically verifiable, renewable credential.

1. Run a 3-minute session

Complete the five-phase behavioral demo. We measure 15 signals from peer-reviewed behavioral science research, run environmental bot-detection (BotD), and verify your typing patterns aren't paste-bursts from an LLM. At the end, you get a signed credential.

Start the demo

2. What you'll get back

  • A signed credential valid for 24 hours — renewable any time.
  • A shareable URL you paste into any verifier (forum, marketplace, policy form) to prove humanness.
  • Zero identity attached. The credential reveals only: you're human, quality tier, and expiry. Nothing else.

3. How it's different from Worldcoin / Pi / Proof-of-Humanity

  • Worldcoin requires an iris scan. We require a keyboard and a mouse.
  • Pi Network anchors to mobile-device identity. We anchor to behavioral physics.
  • Proof of Humanity requires social verification plus a deposit. We require nothing but a browser session.
  • All three are tied to persistent identity. Ours renews in 3 minutes, expires in 24 hours, and produces a fresh unlinkable credential every time.

How it works under the hood: Your browser fetches the SWS public JWKS from
    /.well-known/attention-pubkey.json, verifies the EdDSA signature on the credential
    via crypto.subtle.verify, checks the exp claim (RFC 7519) against the
    current time with a 5-minute clock-skew allowance, and extracts the humanness claim via
    selective-disclosure (VerifiableCredentials.createHumannessPresentation).
    No SWS server is involved in the verification. Anyone, anywhere, any language
    can verify any SWS humanness credential offline, forever.

    
Standards: W3C Verifiable Credentials 2.0
    · RFC 8037 Ed25519 in JOSE
    · RFC 7519 JWT
    · RFC 7515 JWS.
